SPONGE SOAP HOLDER

In the subject of Product Design we were given the task of taking an ordinary object from everyday life and to transform it into an artistic statement. With this in mind, I began looking and playing around with objects from my household.

Subject: Product Design
Material: Kitchen Sponges
Type of Project: ReadyMade with a function

INSPIRATION

At the kitchen I encountered two sponges, which stood so well next to each other by their complementary contrast After a while, I realized they could be used as modular elements to build structures in any shape and height.

BUILDING

After that, I started my ideation phase by building various constructions. In the meantime I had to figure out how stabilize the structure and to find a good use case for the finale result.
